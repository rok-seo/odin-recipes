Pina Colada

Description

A Pina Colada is a cocktail made with rum, coconut milk, and pineapple juice

Ingredients

  • Rum
  • Coconut Milk
  • Pineapple Jucie
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Mix together the rum, coconut milk, and pineapple juice
  2. Blend the mix with ice cubes in a blender
  3. Serve!
