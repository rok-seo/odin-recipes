Loco Moco

Description

A loco moco is a simple Hawaiian dish, comprising of a hamburger patty on rice covered in gravy with a sunny side egg on top.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger patty
  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Gravy

Instructions

  1. Start caramelizing the onions
  2. Wash and begin cooking the rice
  3. Add mushrooms to the onions once they are almost done caramelizing
  4. Once the mushrooms are 80% cooked, add the gravy and let it cook
  5. Cook the hamburger patty
  6. Plate!
