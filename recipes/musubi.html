Musubi

Description

A musubi is a simple Hawaiian dish comprised of spam over rice, wrapped in seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Seaweed
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Begin cooking the rice
  2. Cook spam with soy sauce and sugar
  3. Cut the seaweed into small rectangles
  4. Form the rice into rectangular shapes
  5. Place cooked spam on top of the rice and wrap in seaweed
